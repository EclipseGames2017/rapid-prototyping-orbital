Project: Orbital

A project by UOS MSc Game Dev students

Here is the latest playable version of the project (as of 01/12/2017). Play in-browser to the right, or download for android.

Notes and instructions:

  • The highscore does work, however it is stored locally in your browser, not shared.
  • Use the mouse to drag from any point on the screen. The ball will be fired from the launcher at the angle of your touch.
